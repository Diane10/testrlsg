Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why clean Energy?

    All energy sources have some impact on our environment. Fossil fuels like coal, oil, and natural gas. They do substantially more harm than renewable energy sources by most measures, including air and water pollution, damage to public health, wildlife and habitat loss, water use, land use, and global warming emissions.

  • Why renewable energy?

    Renewable energy sources can be used to produce different form of energy with fewer environmental impacts. It is possible to make electricity from renewable energy sources without producing much carbon dioxide (CO2), the leading cause of global warming.

  • What RLSGL is looking for about clean energy??

    The purpose of RLSGL is to give contribution in reinforcement of the government policies to reach the vision 2020 target through protecting the environment against massive deforestation and soil destruction by increasing the forest cover of the national land area and making ease the pressure on wood biomass. According to the overall objective of EDPRSII, our projects will help to reach on target of 30% for the forest cover of national land area and to reduce the wood energy consumption from 86% to 50% by 2020.In addition, once clean energy is used, it will reduce the air pollution and other problems associated with emission of CO2 in the atmosphere. (global warming and climate change), hence it will increase the quality of life of Rwandans.
